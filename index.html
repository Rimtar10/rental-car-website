Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

What are your rental requirements?

Renters must be at least 25 years old with a valid driver's license and major credit card. International renters must present a passport and international driver's license.

What is your cancellation policy?

Reservations may be cancelled up to 24 hours before pickup with no penalty. Late cancellations may incur a fee equivalent to one day's rental.

Do you offer one-way rentals?

Yes, we offer one-way rentals between our locations for an additional fee. Please contact our reservations team for details.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover) as well as debit cards with sufficient funds.
